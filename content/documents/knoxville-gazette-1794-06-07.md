## The Knoxville Gazette

### Volume 3, Number 23 - June 7, 1794

---

### New Counties Established

<passage id="new-counties">Governor Blount has proclaimed the establishment of several new counties in the territory. The rapid increase of settlements has rendered this division necessary for the convenient administration of justice and the transaction of public business.</passage>

### Blount College

<passage id="blount-college">We are happy to announce that a seminary of learning, to be called Blount College in honor of the Governor, has been established at this place. The institution will afford to the youth of this territory an opportunity for acquiring a liberal education without the expense and inconvenience of sending them to distant states.</passage>

### Advertisement

Wanted: A school master to teach in the new academy. A person well qualified in the Latin and Greek languages, and in mathematics, will meet with liberal encouragement.
