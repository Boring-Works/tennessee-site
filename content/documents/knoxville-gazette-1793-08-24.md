## The Knoxville Gazette

### Volume 2, Number 38 - August 24, 1793

---

### Census of the Territory

<passage id="census-results">The census lately taken shows that the population of this territory has increased to upwards of thirty thousand souls. This rapid growth demonstrates the attractiveness of the country and the confidence of the people in the stability of the government.</passage>

<passage id="statehood-prospect">The increase of population brings with it the prospect of statehood. When the territory shall contain the number of inhabitants required by law, the people will be entitled to form a constitution and be admitted to the Union on an equal footing with the original states.</passage>

### Commerce Report

The trade of Knoxville continues to increase. Several new stores have been established, and goods of all kinds are now available at reasonable prices.
